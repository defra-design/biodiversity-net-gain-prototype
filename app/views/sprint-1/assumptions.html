Assumptions

Register application

  1. Only the landowner or someone with the permission from the landowner will submit an application.
  2. The 'land' will be registered on HM Land Registry.
  3. Applicants will have access to a completed metric calculation tool.
  4. To register a gain site you need to provide a local planning authority reference.
  5. A gain site will have only one associated development (in a local planning authority system).

Public register

  1. Local Planning Authorities want to search by unique ref.
  2. Members of the public want to search by area (postcode or street name).
  3. A gain site record needs a (pin on a) map to visually reperesent its location.
