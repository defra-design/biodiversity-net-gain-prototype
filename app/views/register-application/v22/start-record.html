You'll need:

  • written authorisation from your client if you're applying on their behalf
  • written consent from the landowner or leaseholder to use a biodiversity gain site for off-site gain
  • a completed statutory biodiversity metric file - it must be the same file a local planning authority reviewed with your development's Biodiversity Net Gain Statement

What happens after you send us your information

We will let you know within 6 weeks if:

  • we need more information from you
  • the development project's off-site gains have been recorded or rejected

Withdrawals and refunds

Once we have made a decision on your application you cannot withdraw it or apply for a refund.

You may be able to get a refund if we have not made a decision within 6 weeks.

You can find out more information on withdrawals and refunds in the terms and conditions.
