Use this service to manage biodiversity gains in England as part of biodiversity net gain (BNG).

You can:

  • register a biodiversity gain site
  • record off-site biodiversity gains for a development project
  • record habitats being used for off-site gain by a development project at the same as registering a biodiversity gain site

If you're a land manager you can use your land and register a biodiversity gain site. You'll be able to sell the biodiversity units on your biodiversity gain site.

Development projects can buy the biodiversity units and use them as off-site gains to meet the minimum 10% BNG. Development projects are legally required to record their off-site gains using this service.

We will add approved biodiversity gain sites and linked development projects to the BNG public register.
