What to do if you have no legal agreement

You need to upload a legal agreement that:

  • secures the habitat enhancements on the land
  • is for at least 30 years

This must be either a:

  • planning obligation (Section 106 agreement)
  • conservation covenant

Your file can be a DOC, DOCX or PDF. It must not be larger than 50MB.
