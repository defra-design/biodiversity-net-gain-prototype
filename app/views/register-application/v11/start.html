Register a biodiversity gain site

Use this service to register a biodiversity gain site in England that developments can use to meet at least 10% biodiversity net gain.

Who can register

A landowner, or someone acting on their behalf can register a biodiversity gain site.

What you'll need

To register land as a biodiversity gain site, you'll need:

  • consent from the landowner - if you're not the landowner
  • a legal agreement securing the site for at least 30 years
  • proof of ownership of the land
  • a file showing the boundary of the land
  • a completed Biodiversity Metric (Secretary of State version)
  • a habitat management and monitoring plan
