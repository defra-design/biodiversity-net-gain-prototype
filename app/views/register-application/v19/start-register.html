You'll need:

  • the title deeds or lease agreement to prove ownership
  • consent from the landowner or leaseholder if you do not have proof of ownership
  • a map showing the land boundary
  • a completed Biodiversity Metric 4.0
  • a habitat management and monitoring plan
  • a planning obligation (Section 106) or conservation covenant that secures the site for at least 30 years
  • a local land charge certificate
