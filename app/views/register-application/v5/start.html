You need to provide a legal agreement that links habitat enhancement on the land to a development site.

The legal agreement can be a conservation covenant or a planning obligation.

The legal agreement needs to include the following:

  • the land is in England
  • the landowner or tenant has rights to the land
  • the person or organisation responsible for the enhancement is considered fit and proper
  • the enhancement began 30 January 2020 or later
  • the enhancement will be maintained for at least 30 years
  • the enhancement is measured using a biodiversity metric against a baseline
  • the enhancement follows rules on additionality and stacking including on protected sites

You will also need to:

  • upload your completed biodiversity metric 3.0 calculations
  • upload your management and monitoring plan
  • tell Defra about the landowner and third parties who have an interest in the land
  • provide the boundary of the land
