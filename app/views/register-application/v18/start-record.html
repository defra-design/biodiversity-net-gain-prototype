Guidance

Record off-site gains for a development project


Find out what documents you need and how much it costs to record habitats as off-site gains for a development project.

You'll need:

  • written consent from the landowner or leaseholder
  • a completed Biodiversity Metric 4.0 file – it must be the same file a local planning authority reviewed with the development project's Biodiversity Net Gain Statement

Registration fees

It costs £36 to record off-site gains for a development project. You can pay when you register or within 28 days.

What happens after you send us your information

We will let you know within 6 weeks if:

  • the development project's off-site gains have been recorded or rejected
  • we need more information from you
