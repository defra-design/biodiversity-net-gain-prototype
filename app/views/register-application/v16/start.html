Use this service to record biodiversity gains in England as part of biodiversity net gain (BNG).

You can:

  • register a biodiversity gain site
  • record off-site biodiversity gains for a development project

If you're a land manager you can use your land and register a biodiversity gain site. You'll be able to sell the biodiversity units on your biodiversity gain site.

Development projects can buy the biodiversity units and use them as off-site gains to meet the minimum 10% biodiversity net gain. Development projects are legally required to record their off-site gains using this service.

We will add approved biodiversity gain sites and linked development projects to the biodiversity net gain public register.
