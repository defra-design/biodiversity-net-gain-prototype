You can download the 'Authorise an agent' form. You can then upload it after it's been completed by the landowner or leaseholder.

Or you can upload a document that includes:

  • a written statement confirming the landowner or leaseholder's consent for you to act on their behalf
  • the landowner or leaseholder's name and signature
  • the date consent was given

Your file can be a DOC, DOCX or PDF. It must not be larger than 50MB.
