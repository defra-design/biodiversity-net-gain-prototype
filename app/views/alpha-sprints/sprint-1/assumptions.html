Assumptions

  1. We understand the Local Planning Authorities (LPA) data needs sufficiently for them to use the register when assessing gain plans.
  2. LPA's will want to search by unique ref.
  3. Members of the public will want to search by area (postcode or street name).
  4. LPA's need to see a boundary on a map to confirm the gain site area.

User needs

As an LPA I need to see what biodiversity gain sites are allocated to a particular development so that I can approve the net gain plan.
