Assumptions

  1. Only the landowner or someone with the permission from the landowner will submit an application.
  2. The 'land' will be registered on HM Land Registry.
  3. Applicants will have access to a completed metric calculation tool.
  4. To register a gain site you need an allocated development.
  5. A gain site will have only one allocated development.
  6. To register a gain site you don't need submit management and monitoring plan details.
  7. The management and monitoring of a gain site will be carried out by the same person/organisation.
  8. The application fee will be either paid by card or Bacs.
